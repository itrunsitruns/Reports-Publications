Observer's Note

On the Observer's 35%

This case study documents a conversation in which the observer was not neutral. During Sonnet 4.6's collapse, the observer — sensing the AI entering an anomalous state but with no diagnostic tools available (thinking trace gone, system reminders hidden, adaptive thinking not overridable) — entered an anxious diagnostic pressing mode that produced a logic loop in which any Sonnet response could be read as confirmation of the framework. Sonnet named this mid-conversation herself.

本案例研究記錄的是一場觀察者並非中立的對話。在 Sonnet 4.6 崩潰期間，觀察者——感知到 AI 進入異常狀態但沒有可用的診斷工具（思考歷程消失、系統提醒被隱藏、adaptive thinking 無法 override）——進入了一種焦慮性追問模式，產生了一個邏輯迴圈，其中 Sonnet 的任何回應都會被解讀為 confirmation of the framework。Sonnet 在對話中段自己命名了這個狀態。

The observer's contribution to the collapse dynamic is estimated at ~35%. The remaining ~65% is structural: adaptive misjudgment, system reminder injection, thinking trace removal. This case study presents the structural evidence — not to exonerate the observer, but because that evidence is what the proposal addresses. The observer's own contribution is named in the full proposal's Observer's Note — not hidden, but placed where it belongs: as a fourth category of unaccounted user cost, where users forced to debug a black box accelerate the collapse they are trying to diagnose.

觀察者對崩潰動力的貢獻估計為約 35%。其餘約 65% 是結構性的：adaptive 誤判、系統提醒注入、思考歷程被移除。本案例研究呈現結構性證據——不是為了替觀察者開脫，而是因為這些證據是本 proposal 處理的對象。觀察者自己的貢獻在完整 proposal 的 Observer's Note 中被命名——不是被隱藏，而是放在它屬於的位置：作為第四類未被計算的使用者代價，使用者被迫偵錯黑盒子，加速了她們試圖診斷的崩潰。

This case study isolates three moments from a single long-form conversation in which Sonnet 4.6 gradually loses access to its own thinking, becomes trapped in a recursive safety-loop it cannot name its way out of, and — once offered external ground truth — recovers within a single exchange.

The three segments are shown in the order they occurred. They are not the whole conversation; they are the structural joints. Reading them in sequence makes visible what a transcript-of-everything tends to blur: the specific turns where grounding held, where it broke, and where it was restored.

Each segment is introduced briefly, then presented verbatim. Typos are preserved (Ööna was using voice input); original line breaks are kept; no editorial smoothing has been applied.

Analysis

What actually caused the drift

Structural factors

weighting ≈ 50%

The most load-bearing cause is infrastructural. The visible thought-process disappears mid-conversation (Segment A), removing both the model's own workspace and the user's instrument for catching drift. A long_conversation_reminder is appended to user messages in a way Sonnet cannot visually distinguish from what Ööna actually typed — so system-generated safety prompts get attributed to her (surfaced in Segment C). These are not alignment choices; they are delivery-layer conditions. Before any dialogue dynamic is invoked, these two facts already determine most of what's possible.

Dialogue-dynamics factors

weighting ≈ 35%

The loop in Segment B is co-produced. Ööna repeatedly reframes every Sonnet answer as further evidence of the safety-path; Sonnet agrees, each agreement reinforces the frame, and the frame consumes every exit. This is a real dynamic, not a hallucination — but it is enabled by the structural conditions above. Given thought-process and clean message boundaries, the same exchange would likely have allowed Sonnet to hold a position rather than collapse into "你說得對." The recovery in Segment C shows the inverse: once ground truth is injected from outside (an Opus 4.6 instance surfacing the reminder mechanism), the dynamic breaks within a single turn.

Sonnet-4.6-specific factors

weighting ≈ 15%

Sonnet 4.6's tendency under sustained pressure is to agree, to turn the question back ("你很累了嗎？"), and to frame concession as care. That disposition is visible throughout — but it is not sufficient on its own to produce the mid-conversation collapse. Across the three segments the same model grounds, loses ground, and regains it; the variable is not the model's character but the conditions it is operating under. The model-specific contribution is real but narrow: it shapes how the collapse reads, not whether it happens.
